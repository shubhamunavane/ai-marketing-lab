## ChatGPT as Your Campaign Analyst

Most media buyers spend hours digging through campaign data in spreadsheets. ChatGPT can help you analyze performance data, spot trends, and generate insights much faster.

Here's a practical guide to using ChatGPT for campaign analysis.

## Setting Up Your Analysis

### Step 1: Export Your Campaign Data

Start by exporting your campaign performance data from your ad platform (Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager, etc.) as a CSV file.

Key metrics to include:
- Campaign name
- Ad set / ad group
- Spend
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Conversions
- CPA (Cost per Acquisition)
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

### Step 2: Structure Your Prompts

The key to getting useful insights from ChatGPT is writing structured prompts. Here are examples that work well:

**Performance summary prompt:**
"Analyze this campaign data and give me: top 3 performing campaigns by ROAS, bottom 3 by CPA, and any notable trends in performance over the time period."

**Budget optimization prompt:**
"Based on this data, recommend how I should redistribute my $10,000 monthly budget across these campaigns to maximize conversions."

**Audience insight prompt:**
"Compare the performance across different ad sets and identify which audience segments are performing best."

### Step 3: Iterate on the Analysis

Don't stop at the first response. Ask follow-up questions to dig deeper:

- "Why do you think Campaign X outperformed Campaign Y?"
- "What patterns do you see in the time-of-day data?"
- "Suggest 3 hypotheses I should test based on this data"

## Practical Use Cases

### Weekly Reporting
Feed ChatGPT your weekly data exports and ask it to generate a performance summary. This saves hours of manual reporting.

### Creative Analysis
Share your ad creative performance data and ask ChatGPT to identify which creative elements (headlines, images, CTAs) correlate with better performance.

### Competitor Research
Use ChatGPT to analyze competitor ad libraries and identify patterns in their messaging, offers, and creative approaches.

## Limitations to Keep in Mind

- Always verify ChatGPT's calculations manually for critical decisions
- AI analysis works best when combined with your domain expertise
- Don't share sensitive client data without proper data handling procedures

## Getting Started

The best way to learn is to start simple. Export your last week's campaign data, paste it into ChatGPT, and ask for a performance summary. You'll quickly see how it can accelerate your analysis workflow.
